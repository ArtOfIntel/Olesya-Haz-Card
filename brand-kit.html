01

Logo & Usage

The Olesya Haz logo is the primary visual identifier. It represents a safe, flowing, and professional space. It should be used with sufficient clear space (padding) around it to ensure legibility.

Clear Space & Positioning

Ensure the logo has breathing room. Do not crowd it with other text or graphics.
